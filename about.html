The Channel

Built on a simple
question.

What if you just went? No itinerary, no safety net — just a camera and a one-way ticket to somewhere that made you a little nervous. That's where Go Everywhere Do Everything began.

Stas and Nick met through a shared obsession with getting to places most people only see in magazine spreads. What started as a way to document their own travel grew into something bigger: a community of people who believe the world is best understood from inside it.

The channel covers 40+ countries and counting — surf breaks, mountain passes, desert crossings, cities that don't sleep. Every video is shot, edited, and narrated by the two of them.

The Team

Meet the people
behind the lens.

Co-Founder · Cinematographer

Stas

Stas is the eye behind the camera — drawn to the kind of light that only happens at the wrong time of day in the wrong part of the world. A self-taught cinematographer, he's spent years refining a visual language that's equal parts discipline and instinct.

His approach to travel is to stay longer than feels comfortable, eat what's on the table, and never leave before sunset. Most of the channel's most-watched frames were shot by Stas at 5am when Nick was still asleep.

Co-Founder · Storyteller

Nick

Nick is the one who asks strangers for directions they didn't ask for, and ends up at a family dinner by mistake. He handles narration, writing, and the chaotic logistics of getting two people with too much camera gear to places that barely show up on maps.

He started travelling seriously after a trip to Southeast Asia that was supposed to be two weeks and became four months. That trip didn't make it onto YouTube. Every trip since has.

What We Believe

Travel is not an escape from life.
It is life, accelerated.

We make videos because the places we find deserve to be seen. Not the packaged version — the real one. The one with bad weather and wrong turns and a guesthouse that smells like someone else's adventure.

Every piece of content we put out is a genuine record of what happened. We don't script locations. We don't re-do moments for the camera. We just go, and we bring you with us.
